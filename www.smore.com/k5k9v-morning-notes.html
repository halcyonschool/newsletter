Grade 6 & 7: Hampton Court Palace

Wednesday, June 25th, 9am-3:30pm

Hampton Court Palace

Students in Grades 6 and 7 will be visiting Hampton Court Palace on Wednesday 25th June. We will leave Halcyon at 9am and arrive back to school at 3.35pm.


Students will learn about how the Tudors exerted power and control over their subjects and we will be doing some maths activities relating to patterns, including venturing into the maze! Students will be accompanied by Ms Cooke, Ms Fritz and Ms Zago.


Please ensure you wear comfortable footwear and bring sun-cream, water and a charged iPad. We will have a packed lunch but you want want to bring a small amount of money for gifts or extra snacks.

Grade 8 & 9: "1984"

Wednesday, June 25th, 12:30pm

Northumberland Ave

England

On Wednesday 25 June, the Grade 8 and 9 classes will be heading to the Playhouse Theatre to watch the latest dramatic interpretation of George Orwell's famous text, "1984".


Nominated for a 2014 Olivier Award for Best New Play, and described as "a powerful, visually audacious multimedia production", we know that our Halcyon students will enjoy this re-envisioning of Orwell's masterful dystopian piece.
