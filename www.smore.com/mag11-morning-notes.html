Upcoming Trip: Grade 6 How do we Build a Community to Last?

West Runton, Norfolk Trip

Grade 6


Destination: West Runton Activity Centre

Dates: Monday 31 March - Wednesday 2 April


Depart: Monday 31 March: 0930 (travel by tube and train)

Return: Wednesday 2 April: 1535 (students will be dismissed from school)


Students will need to bring:

  • Topped up Oyster Card (for travel from the school to London Liverpool Street)

  • Students will not require their iPad on the trip;

  • Some petty cash to purchase snacks (£15-20)

  • Any valuables would be the student’s responsibility.


Clothing/item that students will need to pack:

  • A warm, waterproof coat;

  • Sensible walking shoes;

  • Sensible clothes for outdoor activities (that may get dirty);

  • Night clothes;

  • Toiletries bag including toothbrush, toothpaste, soap, hairbrush, etc;

  • 1 bath towel;

  • Socks and underwear;

  • T-shirts or blouses;

  • 1 thick jumper;

  • Trousers;

  • Sturdy shoes and wellies;

  • Large plastic bag for dirty clothes;

  • Gloves, hat and scarf;

  • Drinking water bottle.

Upcoming Trip: G8 Science Museum

Grade 8 will be going on a trip to the Science Museum on Monday 31 March.

Please meet Ms. Fritz & Ms. Cooke in reception at 12.45pm for a brisk walk to the Museum.


You will need to bring:


  • Sensible walking shoes;
  • Raincoat;
  • A charged iPad.
