Reminder: School Photos

Tomorrow, NiceSmile will take official school photographs. These will include individual pictures, as well as group shots of advisory groups and our first official picture of the entire school.


There will be no dress code on the day, however you may wish to dress smartly.

Art Club meets Mandarin....

Come and have a Chinese experience during Art Club on Tuesdays during lunch! The sessions will be led collaboratively with Mei-Ling Lin, and Sue Dance. You will be introduced to Chinese calligraphy and will learn both the art of Chinese calligraphy and Chinese brush painting with watercolour on grass paper. Watch this YouTube video to catch a glimpse of the beauty and history of Chinese calligraphy writing.


You can still come along to Art Club if you wish to continue with your own work too!

Calling all budding filmmakers, editors, directors and producers!

Join a new Community and Service project that seeks to capture life at Halcyon on film for our website and other promotional purposes! To find out more join C&S meeting during lunch on Tuesday at lunchtime. If you are interested in joining, but have another activity, please let Mia know.
