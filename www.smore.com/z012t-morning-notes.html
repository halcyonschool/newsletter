Athletics in Hyde Park

In PHE sessions for Term 3 students in all grades will start athletics sessions in Hyde Park for one of their double PHE sessions.


During the course of term 3 all students will participate in the following events:


- 100m and 200m sprint

- Long distance running

- Team relay events

- Hurdles

- Beep test

- Shot put

- Javelin


In case of rain we will move the sessions indoors either at SLC or in the Stern Hall.


Students are responsible for bringing a change of shoes, their own water bottles and sun screen if appropriate.
