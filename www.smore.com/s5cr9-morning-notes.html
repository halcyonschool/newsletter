Politics Society: Brexit, The Debrief

Wednesday 02 March

TO2 at 1240


Since the announcement of the "Brexit" referendum two weeks ago (due to happen on 23 June, 2016), we've seen the British Pound tumble (£1 was worth $1.38 on Friday, a 30 year low!), a very confused Boris hoarded by reporters while trying to leave his house on a bike, and the beginnings of a divided Conservative party.


Want to know what the Brexit could mean for you as an International student or unsure of what side to support? Then join us in this week's Politics Society as we try to answer the question Donald Trump has posed many times in his campaign of "What is going on??!"
