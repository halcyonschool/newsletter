Halcyon MUN: 18-19 June

We are pleased to announce that Halcyon Model United Nations will be held on 18-19 June. The theme of the conference will be ‘The World Post Millennium Development Goal (MDG)’. Halcyon will have eight delegates and 8 admin staff involved at the conference, these will be selected from students who attend allocated preparation session, which will start after the April break. We will also be accepting applications from Halcyon students to chair the conference and will be looking for four chairs and four assistant chairs. If you have experience of MUN and are interested in chairing you should write a two page letter of application to Gareth Jones outlining your experiences and skills that will help you successfully chair the conference. The deadline for chair applications is Friday 20 March.
