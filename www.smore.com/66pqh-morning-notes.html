Morning Notes

Friday 27 February

Off campus priviledges

There will be two off campus days per week, one for grades 7/8 & one for grades 9/10. These days will vary each week. Students will be asked to sign up for participation at latest by Thursday of the previous week*. Students will sign up with this form.


*Please note that for next week only, students will be able to sign up for participation until 1535 on Tuesday 3rd March (subject to parental permission being received)

Students will not be directly supervised by Halcyon staff during their time off-campus, although spotters will be routinely deployed to ensure students are following the Off Campus Operating Guidelines (see below).


In order to manage catering arrangements, students going off campus will not eat lunch in school and will be expected to buy their own lunch off site (students may not bring packed lunches onto site on these days). For the same reason, students will be expected to go off campus if they have signed up to do so, the school is unable to change catering plans at short notice. Food may not be brought back into school.


Off Campus Operating Guidelines

  • Students must remain within the defined boundaries. (map -link to when finished)
  • Students are expected to behave in a way that brings credit to themselves and the school while off campus.
  • Students should behave in a way that is safe: following road safety guidelines, staying in pairs or small groups, not approaching and refusing approaches from unknown people.
  • Students are expected to return to school by 1315.
  • Students must sign out and in with Reception
  • Students who do not comply with these guidelines may have off campus privileges withdrawn.


Parents must give consent for participation in off-campus lunchtimes by completing the form which will be linked to in the newsletter. Students without parental permission will not be able to participate in off-campus lunchtimes. Please remember only students in grades 7 to 10 may participate.


If you have any questions, please contact Mia.
