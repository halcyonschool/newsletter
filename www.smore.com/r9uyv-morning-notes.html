Upcoming Trip - Grade 6: Highgate Cemetery

Wednesday, Jan. 21st, 9:30am-3:30pm

Swain's Lane

The Grade 6 class are currently in the throes of an exciting English and Design Inter-disciplinary unit on the Gothic genre. Additionally, they have just begun learning about mapping in Geography and will be given an opportunity to apply these skills in an upcoming Field Trip to Highgate Cemetery.


Highgate Cemetery is considered one of England's "greatest treasures", providing students with an opportunity to explore Victorian architecture, Gothic-style catacombs, and an abundance of natural forest.


Students must bring:


  • Topped up Oyster card
  • A warm, waterproof jacket
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Fully charged iPad
  • A packed snack, lunch, and water will be provided by Halcyon.

Introduction to Halcyon's Newspaper Club!

Hello, my name is Maxime Cannamela, I am 13 and I am in Grade 8. Last year, in my old school, I wrote a monthly school newspaper along with my fellow classmates during an extra-curricular activity. This year, for my service and action project, I would like to do the same thing except the newspaper would be released weekly and the club will be during Monday lunchtime in F03 and Friday lunchtime in F03 where the members of the club would meet. The newspaper would include some news articles, fun facts, comic strips and colourings. Anybody interested can join by emailing me on the following email: mcannamela19@halcyonschool.com and I will send you a Google Sheet form to fill in before entering. As soon as one person starts entering the club will the newspapers start being published for the following Monday.


Thank you,


Maxime Cannamela.
