Grade 6 - British Library

Friday, Nov. 7th, 10am-12:45pm

96 Euston Rd

Grade 6 Trip to the British Library "Gothic Imagination" Workshop

November 7th, 2014


Connecting to their next English Unit - "Things that go bump in the night: A study of Gothic brilliance" - the Grade 6 class will be heading to the British Library to explore the new "Gothic Imagination" exhibition.


Tracing over 250 years of the Gothic tradition through two hundred rare objects, the students will learn more about the genre's make-up and why it continues to fascinate. They will see original manuscripts of Mary Shelley's iconic text "Frankenstein", as well as more recent spin-offs of the genre such as Stephanie Meyer's "Twilight" series.



Details:

  • Leaving: 10:00am
  • Return: 12:45pm
  • We will enjoy a packed Halcyon lunch at the British Library.


Students must bring:

1) Charged iPad;

2) Topped-up Oyster Card;

3) Comfortable walking shoes.
