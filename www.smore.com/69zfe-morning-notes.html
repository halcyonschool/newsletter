REMINDER: Sports Day 26 June


Halcyon Sports Day will take place from 1030 on Friday 26 June in Hyde Park. It promises to be a very enjoyable day!


Grades 6 and 7 will compete in the junior events and Grades 8 - 10 will compete in the senior events. All students who participate will receive a Halcyon Sports Day 2015 medal. For both the junior and senior events boys and girls will compete separately.


Students will have the opportunity to compete in the following events:

  • 100m sprint
  • 200m sprint
  • 400m
  • 800m
  • Javelin
  • Shot put
  • 100m hurdles
  • 4 x 100m relay

Gold, silver and bronze medals will be presented to students for all of the above events and the HPC has kindly agreed to offer ice cream to all participants.


In the event of heavy rain we will use the sports halls at Seymour Leisure Centre.

REMINDER: Last Day of School Schedule: Dismissal @ 1400


Friday 26 June is the last day of school at Halcyon with a normal start and Advisory 0845-0900 and dismissal at 1400.


The last day of school promises to be fun-filled with (among other things): Sports Day, quizzes, awards, farewell celebrations and Yearbook signing!

IB Diploma Programme taster day


On Thursday 25 June our grade 10 students will have the opportunity to try of the Halcyon IB diploma programme. That day they will enjoy a test of the key components such as Theory of Knowledge, Creativity, Activity and Service and the Extended Essay. They will also enjoy samples of activities and lessons of the six groups of subjects and to explore University options.
