Morning Notes

Wednesday 3 September

Lanyards Update

Unfortunately, your lanyards will not be ready today. As such, we ask that all students continue to wear their name tag for the time being that you will have collected from Miss Emily at Reception.



Thank you!

Reminder: Stern Hall Renovation arrangements

To all students:

This is a reminder that the Stern Hall will be closed until Monday 15th September due to renovations.


Arrangements for PE are as follows:


PE Lesson:

Grade 8: Wednesday, Period 7 & 8: will be in the Goldsmid Hall. Please meet Mr. Byrne in the canteen at the start of Period 7 to be escorted to the Hall.

Lunchtime activities arrangements:

Lunchtime activities will rotate everyday with the following scheduled for TODAY:


Grade 6:


  • Lunch commences at 1205
  • At 1225 students may go to F01 for games etc with Ms Mills.


Grade 7 & 8:


  • Lunch commences at 1215
  • At 1235 students can go to the Hub with Mr. Bryne for games etc.


Grade 9 & 10:


  • Lunch commences at 1225
  • At 1235 students can go to Goldsmid Hall with Marvin from Fit For Sport and Mr. Jones for table tennis and other activities. Please meet them in the back of the canteen to be escorted to the hall.

Any Digital Questions?

Mrs. Crannell, Halcyon's Digital Learning Co-ordinator is in today. If you are having any troubles with your email or accessing your Google Drive Mrs. Crannell can help you.

Interested in MUN?

A big thank you to all those who have already expressed an interest in attending the MUNISH conference in The Hague. If you have not had the opportunity to let me know you are interested do not worry. On Friday this week we will have an information meeting at Lunch 12.45 in T01. We will discuss the trip and for those still interested letters will be given out for your parents to sign to confirm that you are able to attend the trip. See you Thursday Mr Jones.

Halcyon Days September Trip!

Monday, Sep. 8th, 7:30am to Wednesday, Sep. 10th, 7pm

Various Locations (please see below)

We are very excited to announce that during the second week of Term, all our students will have the opportunity to build community, come face-to-face with nature and develop IB Learner Profile attributes.


Departure: Monday 8 September 2014

Return: Wednesday 10 September 2014


Grade 6 - West Runton

Grade 7 & 8 - Isle of Wight

Grade 9 & 10 - Dearne Valley


Things that students will need to pack:


  • A warm, waterproof coat
  • Sensible walking shoes
  • Sensible clothes (that may get dirty) for outdoor activities
  • Night clothes
  • Toiletries bag including toothbrush, toothpaste, soap, hairbrush, etc.
  • 1 bath towel
  • Socks and underwear
  • T-shirts or blouses
  • 1 thick jumper
  • Trousers
  • Sturdy shoes and wellies
  • Large plastic bag for dirty clothes
  • Gloves, hat and scarf
  • Drinking water bottle.


Please note, many of the activities require arms and legs to be covered. See more information here.
