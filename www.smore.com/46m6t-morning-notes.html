Exciting Appointments at Halcyon!

As we begin the school year, we would like to share with you news of three exciting staff appointments!


Miss MacIver has joined us as our Marketing and Communications Co-ordinator. Arriving from Edinburgh, Miss MacIver will be promoting all things Halcyon! Look out for new posts on Halcyon's Facebook and Twitter accounts.


As well as your dedicated maths/science teacher, Mr Jones will also now be taking on the position of Student Wellbeing Leader! Mr Jones will be developing and leading an approach to promote personal growth and learning for all Halcyon students.


Ms Rosengren has been promoted as Halcyon's Admissions Director and Human Resources Manager. As a first point of contact for the school, Mrs Rosengren is a welcoming ambassador for Halcyon!


Congrats to all!
