Grade 8: Upcoming Trip to Tate Modern

Thursday, May 15th, 12:30pm

Bankside

Date: Thursday 15 May

Depart: 1235

Return: 1535


After a quick lunch on Thursday, Grade 8 will depart school to take the Central Line from Marble Arch tube station to the Tate Modern. This trip will introduce us to the Surrealist art-works. These paintings should give the students inspiration for their ideas in creating a design for a backdrop of "Midsummer Night's Dream" that they are studying in English. They will also have the chance to write a review of the work in relation to the concepts of the play, through the lens of Surrealism.



Students will need:


  • a fully-charged iPad
  • jacket
  • topped-up Oyster card
  • art book
  • drawing pencils


Trip leader: Sue Dance.

Message from Grade 6: International Colour Day!

Halcyon will have an International Colour Day that has been initiated and organised by the 6th Grade. It will take place on Friday 16 May. The 6th graders are all very excited and hope that everyone participates!
