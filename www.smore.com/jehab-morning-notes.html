Student Council Awards Committee: Designers Wanted!

Halcyon's Student Council is excited to announce that it has recently been granted permission by the school to put together a small team of students from Grades 6 - 11 to design and create a set of annual awards. If approved, these awards would be handed out annually to students who have excelled in their endeavours, both inside and outside of the classroom, over the academic year.


If selected, your contributions could become an important part of the Halcyon legacy, as the awards we design today could continue to be handed out for decades to come!


We're looking for creative and imaginative students, if you are interested please fill out this Google Form by Friday 18 March. We're looking forward to hearing your ideas!

What can you do to help the environment? And help our school community become more sustainable?

We want students to lead and participate in many different projects around school to help our school become more sustainable.


We want to educate teachers, students and parents about sustainability; introduce recycling into school; educate as to why we are a vegetarian school and to promote healthy and sustainable travel to and from school.


We also welcome any other sustainability projects you would like to implement.


Come join us in Sustainability Club. First half of lunch (1220-1250) today!
