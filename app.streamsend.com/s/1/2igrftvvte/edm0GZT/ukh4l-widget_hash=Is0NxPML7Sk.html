2. What You Need To Know
Over the coming weeks, we will send you everything you need to know to prepare for the start of school in September. Here are five things we would like you to know right now:

1. Don't worry! Please do not purchase any school-related equipment, materials or IT devices yet. We will send you a list of what you need later in the summer.

2. Within the next few weeks, we will ask you to complete some simple forms to provide us with information to complete school record. This will include your child's medical history, so we recommend that you have their immunisation dates to hand.

3. We want all Halcyon students to have an International Student Identity Card. We will send you further details about how to complete the simple online process and provide you with the necessary school evidence you will need to register.

4. Hot lunches are included in your fees and start on the first day of school (2nd September). 

5. The first week of school comprises three Halcyon Orientation Days (2nd-4th September) followed by two days off (5th and 6th September - when teachers continue with their collaborative planning). We would like at least one parent from each family to join us on the first morning (2nd September). Full details will be sent to you in the summer.
