New Halcyon Bookclub!
Date: Tuesday 10 November
Time: 1400
Location: Borough Barista (downstairs), 60A Seymour Street
Keen and not-so-keen(!) readers are invited to join the new Halcyon Book Club beginning next month. Our theme this year will be UK authors from any era with our first book chosen to be Almost English by Charlotte Mendelson. Listed for the Booker Prize in 2013, it is a story of living in West London mixed with the complications of family, education and social connections. Sound familiar? Should be good!

Please join our club for 'Halcyon Parents + Friends' and we ask only that we all come with the book in hand or on tablet, have made a good attempt to read it as much as possible and that we generously share our opinions over a cup of coffee!

Almost English is readily available at the moment and can be found on Amazon paperback for as little as £0.43 or for Kindle Download. Order it, enjoy the read over half term and we look forward to seeing you on Tuesday 10 November. Sign-up is not necessary but is helpful so that we know how many to expect. Any questions please contact Risa Kay or Cathy Smith.

Digital Photography and The Selfie
Date: Tuesday 10 November
Time: 0945 - 1200
Location: Art Workers Guild, 6 Queen Square, WC1N 3AT
Cost: £25 per person (incl coffee and pastries)
The London Art Salon is offering a lecture on the invention and rise of self-portraiture from humble beginnings as hidden painted faces to the obsessive documenting and displaying of our own digital images. While the "selfie" is being billed as a massive contemporary phenomenon, photographers did not wait for the invention of digital smart phones or selfie sticks in order to turn the camera on themselves. The London Art Salon brings together expert speakers in a series of talks and discussion that cover London's current art exhibitions, events and art market. Registration is essential here. For any questions, please contact Anja Beck.

City of London Architecture Tour
Date: Wednesday 18 November
Time: 1030 - 1230
Meeting Place: One New Change EC4M 9AF
Cost: £30 plus £1.26 surcharge
This is an amazing tour with the former head of planning for the City of London. On our walk around the City of London, we will look first at the immediate setting of St Paul's Cathedral and its transformation over recent decades. Our route will continue via Cheapside, the City's revitalised High Street, towards Bank and the Royal Exchange. The tour will continue through the medieval alleyways off Cornhill and take in Leadenhall Market and the Lloyds Building before visiting the cluster of new office towers which house London's insurance sector. Our walk will then turn north to visit Broadgate and Liverpool Station, finishing on the northeastern corner of the City adjacent to Spitalfields. This tour is organised by From My City. Please sign-up directly on this page for Halcyon so that we can keep track of who's coming. For any questions, please contact Stacey Ciauri.
