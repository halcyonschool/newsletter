3. REMINDER: Sports Day 26 June
Halcyon Sports Day will take place from 1030 on Friday 26 June in Hyde Park. It promises to be a very enjoyable day, please come and cheer on our athletes!

Grades 6 and 7 will compete in the junior events and Grades 8 - 10 will compete in the senior events. All students who participate will receive a Halcyon Sports Day 2015 medal. For both the junior and senior events boys and girls will compete separately.

Students will have the opportunity to compete in the following events:
  • 100m sprint
  • 200m sprint
  • 400m
  • 800m
  • Javelin
  • Shot put
  • 100m hurdles
  • 4 x 100m relay
Gold, silver and bronze medals will be presented to students for all of the above events and the HPC has kindly agreed to offer ice cream to all participants.

In the event of heavy rain we will use the sports halls at Seymour Leisure Centre.

iv. Buddy Program - Remember when you were new to London?

We are looking for families to volunteer to be a 2015/16 Buddy Family. We invite existing Halcyon families to become a buddy family for one of the many new families joining our school community each year. We would like to pair each new family with a “local” family who will help provide a warm welcome to them. A little effort can go a long way for those families moving from both near and far.

As a buddy family, we ask you to do the following:
  • Call or email and welcome your buddy family to the Halcyon community and to London (if from elsewhere)
  • Set up a get-together with the children and/or parents
  • Answer any questions about the school, HPC or living in London
If you are interested in volunteering as a buddy family, please contact Jenny Garcia. Thanks for helping to welcome our new families!
