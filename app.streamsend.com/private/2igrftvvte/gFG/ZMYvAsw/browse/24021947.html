2. The Halcyon Team
As we approach the end of the school year, it is a timely moment to start getting ready to say some goodbyes and thank yous and some hellos and welcomes to members of the teaching team.

As you know, Duncan Partridge will complete his tenure as interim Head at the end of this year and he will be returning to international education consultancy work. Lenny Dutton-Williams will be taking up a new post at Atlanta International School and Celia Bowker will be returning to Washington with her family. We will of course be saying our thank yous and wishing them all well as the Term closes.

With the continued growth of the school, our staff team is expanding considerably next year and we are fortunate to have appointed a group of teachers who are highly experienced, committed to student centred IB learning and come with glowing recommendations. Full bios of new staff will be included shortly on our website but in the meantime, here are the new members of Team Halcyon:

    iv. Lunch Club
    Wednesday 10 June 1200 (noon)
    Kensington Roof Gardens

    Welcome to our new “Lunch Club!” Every month, we will choose a venue, which represents a different country and culture, recommended by parents and friends.     As a result of the growth in the student body, the HPC has decided to introduce Grade Reps for 2015-16. If you are interested in becoming a grade rep, please contact Jenny Garcia.

    xii.
    Parking Signs for Drop-off/Pick-Up
    Halcyon is able to offer parents a laminated parking sign to display on your car dashboard when you need to run into the school for drop-off or pick up. With the displayed sign you are allowed to park on a single yellow line (not double yellow!) outside the school for a maximum of 10 minutes for the purposes of dropping off/collecting your child during school hours. You must be parked right outside the school. If you would like a parking sign, please contact Emily at Reception and provide your car registration plate number. Please note that, in the unlikely event of a parking violation, THE SCHOOL WILL NOT BE HELD LIABLE.
