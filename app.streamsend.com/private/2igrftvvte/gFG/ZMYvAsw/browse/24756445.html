NEW PARENTS: Invitation to Managebac - remember to check your spam/junk
Managebac is an online curriculum and assessment database devised especially for IB students. It enables efficient curriculum planning, assessment and reporting, while eliminating paperwork and enhancing communication to parents and students. New parents will shortly receive an invitation from info@managebac.com inviting you to log in to view details of the curriculum in each subject. The system enables you to view a calendar of major assessment tasks and due dates; see your child’s electronic portfolio of work, including marked assessment tasks and grades; follow your child’s community and service activities and their progress towards their personal learning goals.
On Tuesday 1 September we will have an information station to help you navigate and log on to your ManageBac account, but you may like to log in beforehand and familiarise yourself with the system. If you have any queries regarding this or the curriculum in general, please contact our IBMYP Co-ordinator, Lori Fritz.
