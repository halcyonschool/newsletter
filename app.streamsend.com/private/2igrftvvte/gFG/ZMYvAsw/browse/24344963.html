3. Renovations and Expansion Underway
We are pleased to report that close on the tails of staff training days last week, renovations began at Halcyon on the Mezzanine floor. The WLS Library is being relocated to the Council Room on the first floor and the staff office is being moved and expanded on the second floor to include a work room as well as tea point and lounge. This will allow for our new science and design technology lab and an additional classroom to be built on the Mezzanine floor as well as more space for our growing staff on the second floor. While we are not embarking on a full renovation of the new space we have been allocated on the second floor just yet, we are pleased to also be able to offer a IB Diploma Programme Common Room as well as two new offices, a staff WC and a female student WC.
4. *MARK YOUR CALENDARS* - Back to School Events
Welcome Picnic
Monday 31 August 1400-1700
Location: Hyde Park - open grassy area closest to Marble Arch

Calling all Halcyon families, old and new! To start off the 2015/16 school year, the Halcyon Parent Community will be hosting a "bring your own" picnic in Hyde Park. The picnic will give old and new families the chance to meet each other in a relaxed atmosphere. Halcyon students will be there to help break the ice for new students. Remember to bring your own picnic and blanket! If it is cloudy that morning, we will go ahead, but if it is raining, the picnic will be cancelled.

First HPC General Meeting of 15/16
Friday 4 September 0900-1030
Stern Hall

All parents belong to the Halcyon Parent Community (HPC) and are invited to attend the first meeting of the new 2015/16 school year. Come learn about all the exciting activities and plans that have been organized for the year and share any ideas you may have. The HPC Board brings together enthusiastic parent volunteers to enrich our school community. We organize parent socials, tours, lectures, outings and other interest-based activities. For more information about the HPC, the HPC Board and how you can get involved, please contact Berna Hudson, HPC Chair for 2015/16.

HPC on Facebook
The HPC is on Facebook! Please search for "Halcyon Parent Community" and request to join our closed group. We hope the Facebook page will serve as another communication tool for parents and also as a resource for getting tips and advice on all things London from our well-connected parent community.

The HPC is an all-volunteer group that works to support community life at Halcyon for all students, parents and staff. We welcome any and all support.
