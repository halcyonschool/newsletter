        8. Grade 10 IBDP Taster Day on 25 June
        Date: Thursday 25 June
        Location: Halcyon London International School

        On Thursday 25 June our Grade 10 students will have the opportunity to try out certain elements of Halcyon’s IB Diploma Programme. They will enjoy a taste of the key components such as Theory of Knowledge (TOK), Creativity, Activity and Service (CAS) and the Extended Essay. They will enjoy samples of activities and lessons of the six groups of subjects and to explore university options.

        Students will be learning about new CAS opportunities that Halcyon has made with West London Synagogue. These include working with the homeless and with Syrian refugees. Students will also find out more about the upcoming CAS trip to Tanzania.

        Students will be given the opportunity to participate in a Theory of Knowledge introductory class. During this session, students will be encouraged to develop their critical thinking skills by exploring and unpacking a selection of ToK knowledge questions. In addition to this, students will be given an opportunity to identify and discuss the ways of knowing and areas of knowledge which they will be expected to research and explore in more depth over the two year IBDP course.

        Students will be introduced to the Extended Essay component of the Core Programme and explore “what makes a good research question?” Students will also have the opportunity to look at past examples of Extended Essays and gain an understanding into how this unique aspect of the Diploma Programme is so valuable to universities and future study.

        iii. Buddy Program - Remember when you were new to London?

        We are looking for families to volunteer to be a 2015/16 Buddy Family. We invite existing Halcyon families to become a buddy family for one of the many new families joining our school community each year. We would like to pair each new family with a “local” family who will help provide a warm welcome to them. A little effort can go a long way for those families moving from both near and far.

        As a buddy family, we ask you to do the following:
        • Call or Email and welcome your buddy family to the Halcyon community and to London (if from elsewhere)
        • Set up a get-together with the children and/or parents
        • Answer any questions about the school, HPC or living in London
        If you are interested in volunteering as a buddy family, please contact Jenny Garcia. Thanks for helping to welcome our new families!
