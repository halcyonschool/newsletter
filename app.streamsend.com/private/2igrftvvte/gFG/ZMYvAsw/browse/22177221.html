5. 
Halcyon Lunch Taster
The HPC recently hosted the first ever parent lunch tasting at Halcyon. A small group of parents came in and had lunch exactly the way their children do every day - trays and all! They were accompanied by staff and Board members who were in school that day. Yinka Ewuola from Eagle Solution Services gave a presentation about school food and Halcyon's vision and food culture. "It was delicious and interesting to learn more about the food service", commented one parent. Another parent said "Like everything else at Halcyon, the food service has been developed in a very thoughtful and forward-thinking manner."

The HPC are planning to organise a parent lunch each term should other parents wish to come along. If you would like to have input in the Autumn menu, please add your suggestions to this form. 

You can view the latest Halcyon menu here.
6. Upcoming Trip: Grade 9 to National Portrait Gallery
Grade 9 will be heading off to the National Portrait Gallery on Tuesday morning, spending time at the BP Portrait Award show as well as the main hall of contemporary portraits. This will enable the students to analyse the paintings, make notes, references and connections to their own portraits, which they will be developing in the art studio back at school as part of their unit on "Shared Portraits". 

 Admission is free and we are lucky enough to just make the show before it finishes at the end of the week. 

Students will need their: 
  • topped up Oyster cards
  • jacket
  • comfortable shoes
  • drawing pencils 
  • money to buy a postcard of one of the portraits. 

The school will be providing a packed lunch, but students may want to bring some money to buy a snack in the cafe inside the Gallery.
