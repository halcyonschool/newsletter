6. Halcyon Model United Nations 18-19 June 2015
We are pleased to announce that Halcyon Model United Nations will be held at our school on 18-19 June. The theme of the conference will be The World Post Millennium Development Goal (MDG). Halcyon will have eight delegates and eight admin staff involved at the conference, these will be selected from students who attend allocated preparation session, which will start after the April break. We will also be accepting applications from Halcyon students to chair the conference and will be looking for four chairs and four assistant chairs. If your child has experience of MUN and is interested in chairing they should write a two page letter of application to Gareth Jones outlining their experiences and skills that will help them successfully chair the conference. The deadline for chair applications is Friday 20 March.

      8. New Research on the Bilingual Diploma
      We are pleased to share with you the most recently completed Programme Impact Research: Factors influencing students to earn a bilingual diploma.

      The aims of this study were to examine the context of Bilingual Diploma (BD) implementation in schools, to investigate academic outcomes for BD students and to explore perceptions and factors that encourage or discourage students from pursuing the BD.

      Students reported that key factors encouraging them to pursue a BD included the desire to be multilingual, future career opportunities and school promotion of the BD.

      You can read the full article here.
