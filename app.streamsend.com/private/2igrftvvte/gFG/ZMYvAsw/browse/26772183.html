        Student Organised Colour Run
        Date: Sunday 12 June
        Two Halcyon students have organised an opportunity for the Halcyon community to participate in the London Colour Run. We welcome all members of our community to join in! Students, please check with your parents before completing the registration as there is a cost of £25 per participant and a parent/guardian needs to fill in their section for any participants under 18 years of age (see point 6 below).

        For those who are interested in participating, please go to http://thecolorrun.co.uk/london to sign-up.

        Please note that the team name is "Halcyon". If you need any help, refer to the following steps.

        1. Select the Team Colour Runner
        2. Select the option "Team Member - General"
        3. You will then be asked to register with your email
        4. After which, you will be asked "Who are you registering for this event?"
        5. Unless you are 18, you will have to select the second option "Someone else, below 18"
        6. Parent/guardian, please complete the parent/guardian's information for your student
        7. Proceed to complete the form accordingly
        8. Team Name: search for "Halcyon"
        9. NSPCC Fundraising: Select "Not Fundraising"*
        10. Don't get the Fast Track

        *We are raising funds for the charities that are affiliated with the school, not the one for Colour Run.
        Once you have finished signing up, please complete this form so that we know who is participating.

        If you have any questions, please email Mr Costa.
        Thank you!
        The Halcyon Colour Run Team

        How Do I Deal With Being a Parent of an Adolescent?

        Dates: 08 June

        Time: 1900-2030

        Location: Halcyon, Ground Floor, Hub

        A Halcyon parent discussion group has formed to share our collective knowledge of how to be effective parents to our London teenagers. Discussions pool our experience and share stories of raising adolescents. The group is facilitated by a qualified Life Counsellor and meets once a month. For more information please contact Gabi.Vollenweider@gmail.com.
