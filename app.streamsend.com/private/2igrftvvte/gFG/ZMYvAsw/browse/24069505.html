2. Everyone Welcome for Viewing: Grade 9 Visual Arts and English Interactive Installation based on conflict
Date: Thursday 4 June
Time: 15.00
Location: Canteen

Here is a taste of Grade 9 working in their exciting installation down in canteen in a small room. This is based on their interdisciplinary unit (IDU) in Visual Arts and English called "conflict", which they are expressing in response to the novel "Persepolis" and conflict artists that they researched earlier in the term. Students have created structures to alter the space for their painted surfaces and space for digital animations and videos.

Please come along to see how it finally turns out!

    8. 
    Halcyon Table Tennis Tournament
    Halcyon hosted a team of ICS players from Grades 6-9 for a tournament on Wednesday. This was a mixed event open to boys and girls. Natasha - our coach and a former Olympic player - selected a team of 10 players from her two weekly clubs to represent the school.

    Both teams displayed commendable sportsmanship throughout. There were some very close matches on all 4 tables but Halcyon emerged victorious winning 15 matches while ICS won 6 matches.

    Well done to all who participated! We now look forward to further competition soon.

    Thank you to parent Gary Dunkin for photographing the event.

    ii. *NEW DATE* Lunch Club
    Wednesday 17 June 1200 (noon)
    Kensington Roof Gardens

    Welcome to our new “Lunch Club!” Every month, we will choose a venue, which represents a different country and culture, recommended by parents and friends.
