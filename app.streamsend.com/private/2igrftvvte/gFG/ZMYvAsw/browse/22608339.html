4. Grade 10: Step One Career Testing
Halcyon is pleased to announce that Step One Career Testing for our Grade 10 students will take place during the week commencing Monday 17 November. 

Step One Career Testing will be an integral part of our careers programme and our subject selection counselling process for students selecting their IB Diploma Programme subjects. Each student will complete an online questionnaire (scheduled for 2 ½ hours). The questionnaire attempts to identify students’ strengths, weaknesses, and main areas of interest. Thereafter, students have a 40 minute individual meeting with trained and experienced career counsellors during the week commencing 8 December. Students then receive a detailed written report which can be used by the family as a discussion point leading into the IB Diploma Programme course selection later in the year.

9. National Theatre Costumes
