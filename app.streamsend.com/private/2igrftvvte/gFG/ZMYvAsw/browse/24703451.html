3. TO DO: Purchase a locker padlock
We invite all students to bring their own padlock for their individual lockers. We received valuable feedback from our students that the combination padlocks provided by the school were not the easiest to use, and as such, are welcoming students to bring their own. 

Personal padlocks are the responsibility of the family. Please note that the school reserves the right to remove students' personal locks at any time. If the lock needs to be removed, the school cannot be held responsible for any damage. 

Should students wish to purchase previously used school padlocks, we will be selling them for £1 each, the proceeds of which will be donated to charity.

Please review the shackle dimensions here. Dimensions: A - 7mm B - 19mm C - 21mm.
