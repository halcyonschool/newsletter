1. ISA Testing 7 & 8 October
0900-1200 Monday 7 October: ISA Tests Part 1 - All Grades
0900-1100 Tuesday 8 October: ISA Tests Part 2 - All Grades

The International Schools' Assessment (ISA) tests are standardised tests of reading, writing and mathematical literacy developed by the Australian Council for Education in Research (ACER). They were developed with the international student in mind and strive to be free of cultural bias. At Halcyon, we will use the results to identify any particular strengths or weaknesses of our students in those areas which are tested. The results are just one of the tools we will use to help personalise our curriculum in response to student needs. You will get a report on your child's results late in December, but the tests do not contribute to students' IBMYP term grades and are not included in transcripts.

The ISA tests are 'paper and pencil tests'. Students do not need to bring any special materials but will need a pencil and an eraser and a blue or black pen. Students whose first language is not English may use a translating dictionary. Each test lasts about 45 minutes but the tests are not time limited - students may continue working past the allotted time if they need. There are breaks between each test and students may want to bring some healthy snacks or drinks to refresh themselves during the breaks.

Any questions, please contact Lori Fritz.

6. Halcyon Book Club
All students have now signed up to Good Reads. This is a great website and app where you can write book reviews, take quizzes on books, create book lists, see what books your friends are reading and join book groups. 

Halcyon now has its very own book group. Members will be recommended 4-6 books every few weeks. All students should aim to read at least one of these. The books will be a mixture of fiction and non-fiction and will range in difficulty. Often these books will complement the students' studies too. There will be a competition each time we change the books and the best review will win a prize. 

The group is open to all members of the Halcyon community, including parents. 

If you have any questions, please contact Ms. Jones or Ms. Dutton.
