Halcyon Success at #BETT2017
Today four of our students presented on the Google stage at Bett 2017 along with English teacher Ross McGowan and Digital Learning Leader Jon Neale. This was a fantastic opportunity for our students to showcase their public speaking skills and demonstrate to the EdTech community how they feel digital integration, with a specific focus on the new Google sites platform, has helped to transform their learning. 

Becket and Ellery (G10) represented Halcyon’s Student Council. The Student Council created a website to further develop student voice using Google Sites. They presented to the BETT audience the benefits of implementing a Google site website:
  • Student Council availability to the community 24/7
  • Meetings more attuned to students
  • Noticeable improvement in overall communication amongst Student Council and with community
  • Greater transparency in communications, setting meeting agendas, etc.
  • Easy implementation of Google forms and docs
Overall, Ellery and Beckett emphasised how integrating this digital tool improved their organisation and interaction with the wider student community. 

Ellie and Noah (G11) showcased their fantastic student-led and student created e-magazine - The Halcyonite. The Halcyonite team also opted for a Google site as their platform. Their presentation featured a live demonstration of their first published e-zine. The main benefits of the platform for them are:
  • Easy to use
  • Visually attractive
  • Allows for collaboration - all contributors were able to work on the same platform at the same time
  • Responsive design - works well across all devices- from tablet to smartphone to desktop
Ross McGowan and Jon Neale showcased separately how the new Google Sites platform has been revolutionising the way things are done at Halcyon; both in and out of the classroom. 

Tens of thousands of people from all over the world attended the show; it was a huge honour for Halcyon to present on the Google Stage. 
We also had the pleasure of meeting Jonathan Rochelle, co-founder of Google Docs and Google Drive!
