Save The Date: Explorations Showcase
Date: Friday 20 January
Time: 1430-1600
Location: Halcyon
Explorations form a new and incremental part of Halcyon’s learning experience. Our students now have the opportunity to explore courses of a more vocational nature while collaborating with students from across the MYP, Grades 6-10. Our Explorations form an important part of Halcyon’s approach to life-readying education where students have the opportunity to learn about different areas of interest in a more integrated way. From a wellbeing perspective, students also benefit from supporting one another and learning from students of different ages. Lastly, this gives our teachers a unique opportunity to share additional passions and expertise with our community.

Please come to the Exploration Showcase to enjoy the learning first hand, RSVP here.
Extracurricular Activities - Final Call!
We all highly value our Extracurricular Activities (ECA) offerings as they broaden and enrich our students’ learning and leisure activities here at Halcyon. To provide these in the most efficient manner, we publish the ECA offerings prior to the end of the previous term to allow for timely signups so sessions can begin as soon as students return to school.

Unfortunately, the deadline for signing-up has passed, despite reminders we have had less than 50% response rate. This makes it very difficult for the school to confirm activities that rely on minimum numbers and organise arrangements with outside providers.

May we ask that all parents and students who have yet to review the ECA offerings do so asap here. Please note that students will not be allowed to attend a club unless they have registered in advance. And many thanks to families who have already signed up!

Pending sufficient sign-up, Morning Energy Dance Class and LAMDA will start on Monday 16 January.

Development News: Halcyon's New ‘Inspiring Voices’ Speaker Series
Date: Wednesday 25 January
Time: 1830 - 2000
Location: Halcyon
Cost: £15 per adult and £5 for students and staff 
Halcyon's Development Committee is delighted to present to you the first event in an exciting series of inspiring speakers for students, parents, staff and friends. The focus is on speakers in their 20's and 30's and we hope they might be seen as alumni-type role models for our young school. The plan is to build the speakers series into a valued learning and community building experience for Halcyon and our friends for years to come. And their is the added benefit of supporting fundraising for our Learning Reimagined campaign to develop the second floor into an innovative learning space for our students and for future campaigns.

For our first event, we have two inspiring speakers. Entrepreneur David Benigson is only 28 years old and has already raised over £10 million in funding for his media company Signal. Using Artificial Intelligence (AI), Signal helps companies and brands make more informed decisions by turning down the noise and turning up the relevant signals. David’s journey through school, to university and to co-founding a AI driven start up is inspiring.
 
It is a journey about what he has overcome and not just about what he has achieved. A journey that blends science and art. 

David will be joined by Eric Collins who is an AI expert & COO of Touch Surgery. Eric has advised various start-up founders and has also been part of Obama’s small business advisory. Together their stories show that different paths can lead to success.
 
Drinks and snacks will be served. Please RSVP here and invite friends and students to join you. Get ready for a thought-provoking evening and support Halcyon.
David Benigson
Eric Collins
