British Biology Olympiad Competition
Erin, Ethan, Fra, Noah and Vicky in G11 entered the prestigious British Biology Olympiad competition. They participated in this demanding competition last week by sitting two one hour multiple choice papers that challenged their knowledge of a range of topics covered in the DP Biology syllabus. They were competing with students from all across the UK including some first year university students. We extend congratulations for taking on the challenge!

Young Writers’ Competition
MYP English students recently entered the Young Writers’ latest mini saga competition, called 'Welcome to Wonderland' and we are delighted to announce that 15 Halcyon students are soon to be published writers! Watch this space for the book once it is published on 30 April 2017.

Halcyon’s Model United Nations (MUN) Conference
Date: Thursday 23 and Friday 24 March
Time: 0900-1700
Location: Halcyon
Halcyon’s student-led MUN Conference is fast approaching! MUN is a key part of Halcyon’s student community - it is the largest club at the school!  For the conference, each student is assigned a country and placed in a committee. In these committees, an issue of global importance is debated, with the purpose of creating a resolution that addresses the issue. Students have to debate the issue and create this resolution, from the perspective of their assigned country. More information about the Halcyon conference can be found on the website. 

The theme of our 2017 conference is ‘Cooperation in an Increasingly Globalised World’, and we feel that Halcyon’s parent community has a lot to offer in terms of interesting and relevant speakers. If there are any embassy parents or other parents who would be interested in coming to speak during the opening ceremony on 23 March or the closing ceremony on 24 March, please email the Board of Directors at modelun@halcyonschool.com for more information. 

For Halcyon students who are interested in attending, the sign-up sheet can be found here, and the chairing application form is here. 

Best Wishes, 
Halcyon MUN Board of Directors,
Alexi, Ava, Ellie, Eric, Ethan, Fra, Evy, Kieran, Maggie, Nate, Nathan and Scott.
