Save The Date: Explorations Showcase
Date: Friday 20 January
Time: 1430-1600
Location: Halcyon
Explorations form a new and incremental part of Halcyon’s learning experience. Our students now have the opportunity to explore courses of a more vocational nature while collaborating with students from across the MYP, Grades 6-10. Our Explorations form an important part of Halcyon’s approach to life-readying education where students have the opportunity to learn about different areas of interest in a more integrated way. From a wellbeing perspective, students also benefit from supporting one another and learning from students of different ages. Lastly, this gives our teachers the opportunity to share additional passions and expertise with our community.

Please come to the Exploration Showcase to enjoy the learning first hand, RSVP here.
