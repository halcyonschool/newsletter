Development Update: “Learning Reimagined”
Campaign Reaches Over £90,000
Halcyon is delighted to report that we have received additional gifts and pledges from our very generous community bringing us to a total of just over £90,000. If you missed our letter called “Learning Reimagined” earlier this month, please find it here. Our campaign goal is to reach at least £150,000 before the winter break. Please contact Rita Halbright if you would like to support “Learning Reimagined” through a gift of time, expertise or resources.
Event: Dr Karen Murphy of Facing History on France & South Africa
Date: Wednesday 30 Nov
Time: 1700-1830
Please join Dr Karen Murphy, International Director of Facing History and Ourselves, who will be speaking in an informal setting about Facing History's work in South Africa and France, their education systems and the challenges the two democracies are facing in light of current events. Facing History is a professional development organisation that empowers teachers and students to think critically about history and to understand the impact of their choices. They have a longstanding partnership in South Africa and have engaged in a number of projects in French state schools over the last two years. Students, parents, staff and friends are welcome. Please RSVP here.
