Bringing Lessons To Life
Imagine visiting the bottom of the Barrier Reef, Buckingham Palace or even the surface of Mars all in one afternoon! With Google Expeditions, teachers can take their students on immersive virtual journeys to bring their lessons to life.

Google Expedition teams are visiting selected schools around the UK and Halcyon is one of them. The Google team will be visiting us on Tuesday 13 December. Alongside Google, Jon Neale will be running a session for parents at 1115. It would be wonderful to welcome as many parents as possible to see such a transformative use of technology. Carol Singing
On Wednesday evening 11 Halcyon students took part in a carol concert at Forrester Court Residential Care Home in Paddington. In the build up to the event Claire in Grade 11 held singing practice sessions. The carolling was a fantastic experience for everyone involved. Two highlights from the evening were bringing back the memories of a former music instructor with dementia and enticing one woman who hadn't got out of bed for a long time to come to the lounge and listen to the music. Students also handed out Christmas cards and cookies made by students in the GIN Seeds of Growth exploration.
HPC International Evening at Halcyon
Date: Monday 12 December
Time: 1700-2000
Location: Halcyon
The HPC will be holding the annual school-wide cultural food-fest at Halcyon. This is a fun time to enjoy specialties from all over the world while catching up with parents over a glass of wine. We are asking for contributions in the form of food and child friendly activities. Please contact your Grade Representatives or Berna Hudson for information on how you can contribute to this celebration of our school.

A Halcyon Winter Wonderland
Date: Thursday 15 December
Time: 1515 - 1600
Students are holding a Winter Wonderland celebration at Halcyon and are inviting parents to participate or just support them by attending. 

There will be stalls, face painting, music, a woolly jumper competition and much more. All in aid of fundraising for Mary’s Meals, one of Halcyon’s designated charities.

If you would like to participate, whether it’s running a stall, bake sale, help decorate the hall or donate to the jumble sale, please email Joann Ranson or Lori Fritz who are helping the students with this fantastic event.

If you want to join us as a guest, we hope to see you in your best festive jumper and join in the fun!
