Three vital components of Halcyon's creation are progressing well: our first students have enrolled; our first teachers have been appointed and our building renovations are taking shape.

Renovations Are Underway!

We're excited to report that the renovations to our building in Seymour Place are right on schedule. Our talented team of architects, builders and contractors are working with our Founding Director, Terry Hedger, and the Board to ensure that Halcyon will live up to our dreams. The renovations are taking place over five floors and are due for completion in August.  See Progress >>

The Halcyon Team

We have been inundated with applications to join the Halcyon teaching team. It has been a difficult but enjoyable task whittling down a world-class shortlist. Once we have appointed our final two team members (part-time Arts and PE teachers) we will be announcing all our appointments. View Vacancies >>

Our Global Community

Students joining Halcyon must represent our multicultural world and be internationally-minded. It is no surprise therefore that our first classes are wonderfully diverse, representing nationalities from all around the world. We've started a google map to track our new global family. Explore Map >>
